About Sharks

Sharks are a diverse group of marine animals known for their unique characteristics:

  • Belong to the class Chondrichthyes and are cartilaginous fish
  • Exist in a wide range of sizes, from the small dwarf lanternshark to the massive whale shark
  • Have streamlined bodies and powerful jaws filled with rows of sharp teeth
  • Play a vital role in marine ecosystems as apex predators, maintaining balance in the food chain
  • Exhibit various hunting strategies, including ambush, scavenging, and long-distance migration

Sharks have captivated human fascination and are crucial for maintaining healthy ocean ecosystems.
