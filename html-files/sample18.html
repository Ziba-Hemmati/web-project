About Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. is a renowned technology company known for its innovative products and services:

  • Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne
  • Headquartered in Cupertino, California
  • Creates iconic products like the iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch
  • Offers a range of software and services such as iOS, macOS, iCloud, and Apple Music
  • Known for its sleek design, user-friendly interfaces, and ecosystem integration

Apple has had a significant impact on the technology industry and continues to be a leader in innovation.
