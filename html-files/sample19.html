About The Walt Disney Company

The Walt Disney Company is a global entertainment and media conglomerate:

  • Founded in 1923 by Walt Disney and Roy O. Disney
  • Headquartered in Burbank, California
  • Known for creating beloved characters like Mickey Mouse and iconic films like "Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs"
  • Operates theme parks and resorts worldwide, including Disneyland and Walt Disney World
  • Produces and distributes movies, TV shows, and digital content through Disney Studios and Disney+

The Walt Disney Company has captivated audiences for decades, enchanting both children and adults with its magical storytelling and immersive experiences.
